Bacchon Ko Live Accuracy Ka Khel Kaise Samjhein:

  • Task 2 (Data Split Effect): `Train/Test Split` slider ko ghumakar dekhein! Jab aap Split ko kam karenge (e.g., 20/80), to model ko data kam milne ki wajah se **Accuracy Automatically Crash** kar jayegi (**Underfitting**).
  • Task 3 (Feature Reduction Effect): Features slider ko 30 se kam karke 15 ya 10 par laayein. Aap dekhenge ki accuracy thodi bohot drop hogi, lekin jaise hi aap bilkul 5 features chorhenge, accuracy bohot buri tarah niche gir jayegi kyunki model ke paas zaroori features khatam ho jayenge!
  • Accuracy Badhana/Ghatana (Overlap Testing): Agar aap dono classes ke points ko door-door banayenge, to accuracy **100%** aayegi. Agar aap points ko aapas me mix (overlap) kar denge, to boundary par noise badhne se accuracy automatic kam ho jayegi!
