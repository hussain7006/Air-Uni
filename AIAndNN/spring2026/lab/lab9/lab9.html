Lab 09/10: SVM Interactive

SVM Core Concept

Dosto aur pyaare students! Pehli baar dekhne par Support Vector Machine (SVM) thoda mushkil lagta hai, par isko bilkul aam kahani ki tarah samjhein. Yeh ek aisi smart algorithm hai jo do alag-alag data groups ke beech sabse behtareen aur khuli boundary tay karti hai.

1. The Basics Foundations

Hyperplane (The Boundary Wall)

Concept 01

Do classes ke beech mein jo ideal separating line ya decision boundary banti hai, use Hyperplane kehte hain. Yeh bilkul do dushman qabilon ke beech ki partition wall hai.

Support Vectors (Border Guards)

Concept 02

Woh critical data points jo boundary ke bilkul paas hote hain. Agar yeh points apni jagah se hil jayein, to poori hyperplane ki geometry badal jayegi. Poora model inhi par tika hota hai!

Margin (The Safe Gap)

Concept 03

Hyperplane aur sabse qareebi point (Support Vector) ke beech ka jo khali space ya gap hota hai use Margin kehte hain. SVM ka main mission is margin ko **maximize** karna hai.

Kun Use Karte Hain Kernel Trick?

Har bar data seedhi line se alag nahi hota! Agar Red points bilkul center mein hon aur Green points unke charo taraf hon (Circular Setup), to koi bhi straight 2D line fail ho jayegi.

Formula applied: Z = X² + Y²

The Jadoo Effect: Kernel trick low-dimensional non-separable space ko mathematically high-dimensional space (3D hawa) mein transform kar deti hai. Jisse points apni radius ke hisab se height par chale jate hain aur beech mein ek flat separating plate rakhna aasan ho jata hai!
