Ordinary Least Squares (OLS)

OLS aik aisa tareeqa hai jo linear regression model mein unknown parameters (weights) ka andaza lagata hai. Ye asal qeemat aur model ki batai hui qeemat ke darmiyan 'squared difference' ko kam se kam karta hai.

Error Minimization

Bunyadi Formula: y = Xθ

y = X θ
y: Price Vector
X: Data Matrix
θ: Weights (Target)
