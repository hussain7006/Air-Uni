Perceptron Kya Hai? 🧠

Perceptron AI ka ek "Single Neuron" hai. Iska kaam sirf itna hai ke Inputs ko dekhe aur bataye ke result "HAAN (1)" hai ya "NAHI (0)". Yeh bilkul aik boundary line ki tarah hai: ek taraf positive, dusri taraf negative.

Step 1: Sum = (x1*w1) + (x2*w2) + b
Step 2: If Sum > 0 Output = 1 Else 0
