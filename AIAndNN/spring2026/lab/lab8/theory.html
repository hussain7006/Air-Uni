Knowledge Representation & Logic

Knowledge Representation

How AI models the world. It’s like a human brain categorizing facts: "Fire is hot" & "Ice is cold."

// Representing Fact
is_hot(fire).
can_burn(fire, skin).

First Order Logic (FOL)

The "math of sentences." It uses quantifiers like ∀ (For All) and ∃ (There Exists).

∀x (Man(x) → Mortal(x))
Every man is mortal

Temporal Logic

Handling time-based facts. "If X happens now, Y will eventually happen in the future."

PastNowFuture

Expert System Shells
